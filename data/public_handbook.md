# Employee Handbook (Public Excerpt)

## Office Hours
Our standard office hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday.
Employees may request flexible scheduling with manager approval.

## Dress Code
We maintain a business casual dress code. Client-facing roles should
dress formally on meeting days. Fridays are casual dress days.

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Full remote
arrangements require director-level approval.

## Company Holidays
The company observes 12 paid holidays per year, including New Year's Day,
Independence Day, and a winter break from December 24th to January 1st.

## Contact Information
For general HR questions, contact hr@company.example.com.
