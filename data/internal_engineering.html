Deployment Process

All production deployments go through the CI/CD pipeline. Code must pass unit tests, integration tests, and a manual QA sign-off before merging to the release branch. Deployments happen every Tuesday and Thursday at 2 PM UTC.

Architecture Overview

Our backend runs on a microservices architecture with services communicating via gRPC. The primary data store is PostgreSQL, with Redis used for caching and session storage. All services are containerized with Docker and orchestrated via Kubernetes.

On-Call Rotation

Engineers are on-call for one week at a time, rotating every Monday. On-call engineers must acknowledge alerts within 15 minutes during business hours and 30 minutes outside business hours.

Code Review Standards

All pull requests require at least one approval before merging. Critical system changes require two approvals, including one from a senior engineer.
